1) Purpose / Objective

Explore who tops the earnings list, how pay splits between on-field and off-field, and how patterns vary by sport and market—so brands can target partnerships with the highest ROI.

Tools: Tableau Public Data: Athlete, Sport, Country, Year, Total, On-field, Off-field

Executive Summary

Pool & Mix. In this Top-50 snapshot, athletes earned $3.44B total: $2.36B (68.6%) on-field and $1.08B (31.4%) off-field. Earnings are concentrated: the top 10 capture 32.4% of the pool.

Sport Landscape. By payout share: Basketball ~29.0%, Golf ~24.1%, Soccer ~16.7%, Football ~16.0%. These four account for ~86% of total earnings.

Endorsements. Off-field dollars dominate in a few niches: Tennis (~99.7% off-field share), Soccer (~41.7%), Basketball (~36.5%). Use these for lifestyle/brand-building campaigns.

2) Methodology

Data Overview

  • Records: 50 athletes (Top-50 snapshot); fields include Rank, Athlete, Sport, Total, On-field, Off-field.
  • Missing values: None across key fields.
  • Reconciliation: For every row, Total = On-field + Off-field.
  • Distribution: Median Total ≈ $57.7M (mean ≈ $68.8M); off-field has a long right tail.

3) Key Takeaways

Insights & Actions

  • Concentration. Total payouts = $3.44B; top-10 capture 32.4%. Action: focus premium partner scouting on this decile and look-alikes.
  • Sport mix. Basketball ~29.0%, Golf ~24.1%, Soccer ~16.7%, Football ~16.0%, Boxing ~4.7%, Tennis ~4.1%, Auto Racing ~3.8%, Baseball ~1.7%. Action: budget by opportunity size; over-index where brand fit is strongest.
  • Endorsement power (by sport). Tennis ~99.7%, Soccer ~41.7%, Basketball ~36.5%, Golf ~23.7%, Football ~20.4%. Action: use endorsement-heavy sports for awareness & lifestyle campaigns.

Deeper Analysis

Endorsement economics. Off-field income is 31.4% of the pool, but highly skewed: a small group of global icons captures most sponsorship value. Combine the “Off-field Share” with sport filters to shortlist authentic brand partners.

Sport strategy. Basketball + Golf exceed half the pool. Soccer, while third by payout, offers unmatched global reach; its ~41.7% off-field share and star-density (Ronaldo/Messi/Mbappé) make it ideal for international campaigns.

Portfolio balance. On-field-dominant profiles deliver performance credibility; endorsement-dominant profiles deliver lifestyle reach. Use the dashboard’s athlete view to balance risk and return.
